Do NOT use em dashes (—) in controversy entry titles. The "Claim — Detail — Quote" pattern became formulaic and made every entry feel AI-generated.

**Why:** The user noticed titles all followed the same structure and wanted them to feel more like real newspaper headlines.

**How to apply:** Write titles as natural sentences or phrases. Lead with the most striking fact. Include the key quote or number. Could this be a real headline in a newspaper? If it has 2+ em dashes, rewrite it.
