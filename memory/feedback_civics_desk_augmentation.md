When adding new controversy entries, also check The Civics Desk (`the-civics-desk/articles/`) for explainers that should be updated with new information.

**Why:** The Civics Desk explainers need to stay current. A new Iran controversy might update a timeline or add context to an existing explainer. The tracker, research site, AND Civics Desk are all part of the same ecosystem.

**How to apply:** After the standard augmentation check (tracker sync + research page augmentation), also scan Civics Desk articles for relevance. If unsure whether an update is justified, ask the user rather than skipping or guessing.
